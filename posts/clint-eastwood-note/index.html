Short note about Clint Eastwood

Clint Eastwood has been an absolute inspiration and hero to me (and for the longest time). When I try explaining my reasoning to suprised people, i'm never able to frame it properly. Do I like his acting in spaghetti westerns ? Yes I do, but that's not exactly it. Do I like his elegant narrative directorials ? I love them, but that's not it too. Both of these reasons are simplistic and don't hit the nail exactly about why I revere Mr Clint Eastwood.

It’s something about his personality. A 94 year old man still directing amazing movies 1 !! 20 mostly great movies directed in the last 25 years 2 !! I really do hope I have this kind of energy in my 80’s and 90’s in life, you definitely don’t see this in most people. Also how many folks have a long, storied 65-year acting career, from “Good, Bad and Ugly”(1966) to “Million Dollar Baby”(2004) to “Cry Macho”(2021). There’s something in his acting, the characters he plays feel human. There’s something with his directing too where you go that’s definitely a Clint Eastwood movie. For one, the story is solid and the characters drive the script and the movie. Most of his movies deals with flawed heroes dealing with morality in a way that isnt preachy to audience.
